FAQs

For the seeker within you.

Who is organizing Open Source Day?

Open Source Lab of Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering is responsible for this event. This event was made possible due to the immense support of the management and the Dept. of Computer Science and Engineering of Vidyavardhaka College of Engineering.

Who can participate?

Everyone is welcome to apply, be it students, professionals or turing-test certified androids. If you are under 18 years of age, we’ll need a parental consent form.

What do I get by attending the event?

Enhance your idea about Open Source and meet like minded people.

and a great way to learn, network, get involved, and have fun.

Do we need to bring any equipment for Workshops?

Each workshop will share its own guidelines and pre-requisites. Please refer to the same to figure out—if you need to bring your laptop or not.
